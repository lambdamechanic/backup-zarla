Frequently Asked Questions:

What will my Zarla website look like?
Create it and see! It only takes a few seconds for our AI to generate your custom website. Once it’s done, you’ll be able to scroll through it and see if there’s any changes you’d like to make.
Why is Zarla so much easier than other website builders?

Our AI writes all your text, finds the perfect images, and designs a completely finished website for you in under a minute. Other website builders take hours of your time (if not days or weeks!) and require you to be your own writer, designer, and troubleshooter.

If you’re not already a web designer, that’s a pretty impossible task! 😅

With Zarla, anyone can have a beautiful, finished website - period.

How much does a Zarla website cost?
Our pricing is easy too! It only costs $9 per month, and there’s no contract required - so you can cancel any time.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! You can use our AI website builder to design your site for free. You only pay when you want to publish your site and go live.
Can I choose my free domain for my website?
Yes! Once you’re a paying member just contact our support team and we’ll set it up for you. If you already own a domain, that’s not a problem either - our team will work with you to connect it to your website.
Can Zarla create my website in another language?
Ja, sí, oui! Our AI can automatically translate everything on your website into the language of your choice. Let us know what language you’d like and we’ll make it happen!
Will my website be added to Google?

Yes it will. Your website will be optimized for SEO right from the start, but please keep in mind that it can take up to a few weeks for Google to add your website to its search results.

We recommend using your physical business address on your website and creating a Google Business Profile so that Google will also add your business to Google Maps and local search results.

Can I change the text and images generated for my website?
Yes! Just use the mobile editor to make your changes, or ask us to do it for you. If you have specific image requests, please contact our support team for additional help.
Can I integrate apps and other services with my Zarla website?
Probably, but it depends on the app. Please contact our support team and we’ll let you know!
