How do I create a red and white logo?

  1. Think about the message you'd like your logo to convey.
  2. Research existing red and white logos and see which icons and fonts look good in those colors.
  3. Use a logo maker to generate some unique designs.
  4. Experiment with shades of red combined with white to see what looks best.
