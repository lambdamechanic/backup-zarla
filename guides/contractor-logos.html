FAQs:

How do I create a logo for my contractor company?

  1. Review your business plan for ideas that you would like to convey in your logo.
  2. Research your top competitors' logos for ideas.
  3. Use a logo maker to experiment with appropriate icons, colors, and fonts.
  4. Create a few options and ask friends and family for advice.
  5. Tweak your best design based on the feedback and download it.
