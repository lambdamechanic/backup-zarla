What makes a logo sensual?

A sensual logo shows just enough beauty and romance to tempt the viewer to imagine romantic scenes, without being smutty or explicit. They often use pink, purple, and black to convey fantasy and romance, as well as stylish typefaces that denote a premium and trustworthy brand.
