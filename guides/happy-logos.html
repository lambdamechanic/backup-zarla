What makes a logo design happy?

A happy logo design will use imagery, colors, and fonts that are friendly to convey a sense of upliftment, joy, and enthusiasm. Warm tones are associated with summer and do a great job of symbolizing optimism. Icons that incorporate smiling faces, flowers, and playful characters are also good for adding happiness to a logo.

What are the best fonts for a happy logo?

A font can do a great deal to add happiness to your brand's identity. Handwritten typefaces, such as Gochi Hand and Finger Paint, can give your logo a personable and approachable feeling. The bubbly lettering of the Luckiest Guy and Ranchers typefaces also work well to convey positive energy.
