How do I create a great beauty and hair salon logo?

Think about your brand and the symbols or colors you'd like to associate with it. For example, if you aim to provide clients with a relaxing experience, use a nature-inspired icon, such as flowers or butterflies. Similarly, if your hair salon has a fun aesthetic, use bold colors like red or pink to make an eye-catching statement.
