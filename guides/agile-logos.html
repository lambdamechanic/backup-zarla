FAQs:

What makes a logo agile?

An agile logo is one that communicates customer-centricity, adaptability, and a shared organizational vision through the effective use of icons, colors, and typography. An agile logo tells the world that there's something different about your approach to business.

How do I create an agile logo?

  1. Think about what associates your brand with openness, flexibility, and collaboration.
  2. Think of symbols that represent your company's offerings.
  3. Use a logo maker to come up with a few ideas for your agile logo.
  4. Pick your favorites and tweak them until you're happy.
  5. Use the feedback of family and friends to narrow your final selection.

Why should my agile logo be in PNG format?

If you save a logo or any other image in a PNG format, it can be used on a variety of digital platforms. Unlike JPEG images, PNG images can be saved with a transparent background, making them ideal for online use. The PNG format allows you to scale your agile logo to fit different sized mediums without losing any quality.
