  2. Think of symbols that represent your company's offerings, or words that associate with creativity, beauty, self-expression, or more.

What is an artistic logo?

An artistic logo emphasizes design and recognizes that appearances are important. It makes visual choices that convey a brand identity in the most stylish and visually striking ways possible, often using vivid colors and fancy typefaces to achieve this.

Should artists have logos?

Yes. Fine artists, musical artists, solo artists, and many others all have a unique symbol that brands their work. Fine artists might use their signatures as logos, whereas bands might stick their initials on their guitars, drumsets, and other equipment.
