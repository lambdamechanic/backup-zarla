What are some ideas for a cool Mexican brand logo?

One idea for a cool Mexican restaurant logo is to color a taco in green and red. These colors mimic the Mexican national flag but also the colors of green and red peppers. Take a look at our collection of logo ideas for inspiration.

How can I come up with creative Mexican food logo ideas?

Creative logos come about by using design elements from different disciplines to produce something new. Take what you know about Mexican culture and cuisine, then blend it with some aspect of your restaurant that is unique to you. This is how you create a personal, creative logo.
