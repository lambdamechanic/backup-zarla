FAQs:

How can I make my logo look luxurious?

  1. Consider cooler tones for your logo design, such as greens, blues, and grays, which impart a sense of professionalism. Alternatively, a palette of muted tones with one bright color can also create a harmonious effect.
  2. Choose a serif or script typefaces, such as Playfair Display or Parisienne, which denotes elegance and exclusivity.
  3. If you wish to forgo icons altogether, a monogram of your initials or full name would be understated but elegant.
  4. Use our free logo maker for inspiration.
