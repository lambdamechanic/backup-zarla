Quality Pressure Washing of Houston

https://www.qualitypressurewashingofhouston.com/

Houston, Texas, U.S.

Quality Pressure Washing of Houston is a family-owned business that specializes in the "soft washing" technique and pressure washing to clean both residential and commercial properties. The site is well-organized with an intro to the business, prominent CTAs, a description of their services and techniques, FAQs, and client testimonials — perfecting guiding the visitor through everything they need to know about the business.

Their gallery shows before and after images that can be expanded for clearer visibility, and their contact details and free quote CTAs are prominently displayed on every page.

Pros and Cons

The site guides the visitor through all the necessary information that would be needed on this pressure washing business and also features an industry-related blog and social media links. The logo and color palette are consistently used throughout the site, enhancing the branding for memorability.

WAVE reports a number of empty links, missing form labels, and contrast errors on the site, which impacts legibility, as well as the accessibility of the site for assistive technologies. The "About" page needs a more in-depth description of the business as well as photos of the owners to create a personal link with customers.

Desktop Review

The desktop site welcomes visitors with a short description of the business and its services and two prominent CTAs. However, the text blocks would benefit from a darker color palette to increase the contrast to the background, especially as some text blocks are fairly long. The desktop site scores well on Google's PageSpeed Insights, with an accessibility score of 93/100 and a performance score of 85/100.

Mobile Review

The mobile site is well-optimized for the smaller screen with a different hero image than the desktop site — one more suited to the available space. The hero banner also forgoes the introduction to the business and focuses instead on the CTAs. The introduction following the hero banner shows the same poor quality as on the desktop site, as do certain other text blocks on the mobile site.

While the photos in the gallery cannot be enlarged like on the desktop site, they show to better advantage on a mobile screen. The mobile site scores 47/100 for performance on PageSpeed Insights, due to slow loading times.

Canadian Pressure Washing Services

https://canadianpressurewashing.ca/

Calgary, Alberta, Canada

Canadian Pressure Washing Services is an industrial cleaning company that pressure washes heavy equipment, fleet equipment, railcars, sidewalks, drive-thrus, and parking lots. The site features a modern minimalist design with only the necessary information present and minimal use of CTAs.

The gallery is small and shows some works-in-progress, and the list of services features in-depth descriptions of each. Navigation of the site is simple, with just links for services, the gallery, and contact information. A list of the service areas and social media links are also included in the footer.

Pros and Cons

WAVE reports a single contrast error on the site. The simple design of the site allows visitors to find basic, yet necessary information, including contact details and a free estimate contact form.

The site is only semi-compliant with assistive technologies, according to accessScan. Social media links and the service area list could be made more prominent, and the lack of customer reviews and an "About Us" section is noticeable.

Desktop Review

The desktop site immediately highlights the business niche with the hero banner showing images of pressure washing examples. Text blocks and images are well balanced on the desktop site, with plenty of white space easing the legibility of the site. Confirming the results of our real-world tests, Google's PageSpeed Insights awards this site a performance score of 92/100.

Mobile Review

PageSpeed Insights notes slow loading times and large content on the mobile site, lowering the performance score to 53/100. In our real-world tests, the mobile site performed well, loading in a timely fashion and displaying high-quality images. The hero banner is resized for the smaller screen, leaving space for the free estimate CTA to stand out and catch the eye.

Eco Pressure Clean

https://eco-pressure-clean.co.uk/

Edinburgh, Scotland, U.K.

Eco Pressure Clean is a domestic and commercial cleaning company that has over 10 years of experience in pressure washing. The site focuses on legibility with large images and large font sizes, and it also emphasizes the different services with images of what each entails.

Prominent CTAs help visitors to move around the site, while a list of recent commercial customers and a page of client testimonials build customer trust. The contact details in the floating top menu and in the footer ensure customers have the necessary information to request a free quote.

Pros and Cons

WAVE reports a small handful of errors on the site, including one empty link and some contrast errors. The authentic before and after images included on each service's dedicated page are a great incentive for potential customers.

The site is only semi-compliant with assistive technologies. Large text blocks can overwhelm the visitor and the lack of social media links and an "About Us" page is noticeable. The site should also introduce the team to potential customers.

Desktop Review

Initially, the desktop site looks crowded, with the hero banner highlighting three images with no spaces between them, but the rest of the site makes great use of white space to create easy legibility. The floating top menu could be larger, to complement the font size used on the rest of the site. PageSpeed Insights rates the desktop site highly, with 91/100 for accessibility and 96/100 for performance.

Mobile Review

The mobile site has been well-optimized for smaller screens, and the vertical layout improves the visual aesthetic of the hero banner. However, some of the service headings further down the home page have not been resized as well as the images, and some words run over to the next line.

Due to the size of the text and the length of the text blocks, visitors to the mobile screen will need to scroll a lot to reach the bottom of the page, so a back-to-top button would be beneficial. With the larger content, the mobile site scores 76/100 for performance on PageSpeed Insights.

Brisbane Pressure Washing

https://www.brisbanepressurewashing.com.au/

Brisbane, Queensland, Australia

Brisbane Pressure Washing offers commercial, industrial, school, and residential cleaning around Brisbane and the Golden Coast. The site immediately highlights the contact and free quote CTAs, while also providing descriptions of the different services and a high-quality before-and-after gallery. The menu offers clear navigation that is repeated in the footer. The FAQs and live chat are stand-out features.

Pros and Cons

WAVE reports only two empty links. Clear CTAs, authentic photos, and well-placed contact information make this site shine among competitors. The consistent use of the blue and orange color palette and the large logo is perfect for brand memorability.

The site is only semi-compliant with assistive technologies and WAVE reports a large number of contrast errors on the site. The "Locations" page involves a long list of towns and suburbs that is overwhelming and could be better presented.

Desktop Review

The desktop site is well laid out with a grid pattern that creates visual interest. Highlighting the quote CTAs in orange draws attention to them on the busy desktop site where large, high-quality images capture the focus of the visitor. PageSpeed Insights notes that these large images lead to slower loading times, resulting in a performance score of 75/100.

Mobile Review

The mobile site has a unique design in that the menu is split into a traditional bar menu in the header of the site and the more usual hamburger menu below this bar. As on the desktop site, the hamburger menu includes links to the various services, while the first menu focuses on the business.

The color palette appears brighter on the mobile site, and the vertical layout ensures that each image and text block gets individual attention. PageSpeed Insights reports slow loading times on the mobile site, leading to an overall performance score of 53/100.

Cape Pressure Cleaning

https://capepressure.co.za/

Cape Town, Western Cape, South Africa

Cape Pressure Cleaning is a pressure washing company based in Cape Town with over a decade of experience. They focus on quality, affordability, and attention to detail to build client satisfaction. The site features a minimalist design that prioritizes compact text blocks with concise information to limit the scrolling time of visitors.

With a list of the services on offer and a free quote CTA right in the hero banner, the business grabs attention from the get-go. The contact details are prominent, along with a Google map of the locations they service, but the social media links could be more visible.

Pros and Cons

The minimalist design and simple navigation ensure customers find all necessary service information and contact details. CTAs are clear and well-placed, encouraging visitors to request a free quote and read more information on the various services.

accessScan notes that the site is not compliant with assistive technologies and WAVE reports a number of contrast errors, empty headers, and empty links on the site. The site lacks customer reviews and would benefit from listing awards to reinforce the heading of "best high-pressure cleaning service."

Desktop Review

The desktop site welcomes visitors with great use of white space, compact text blocks, and high-quality images. The sticky top menu offers simple navigation along with a quote CTA and phone number. However, the phone number includes a link that is better suited to a mobile site, as is the chat feature which works through WhatsApp. Contrary to the results of our real-world tests, Google's PageSpeed Insights reports slow loading times, leading to a performance score of 56/100.

Mobile Review

The large hero banner is not adequately resized for the mobile site, and is almost completely overtaken by the list of services and quote CTA — not necessarily a bad thing, as the services and quote CTA are more important information. The chat feature is much better suited to mobiles through the WhatsApp application.

The menu, phone link, and chat link are neatly hidden in the hamburger menu, and the minimalist design offers a great user experience. However, PageSpeed Insights reports that large content and slow loading times lowered the performance score to 29/100.
