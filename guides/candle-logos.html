FAQs:

How can I create a luxury candle logo?

  1. Use a logo maker to get a few ideas.
  2. Choose a creative icon that matches your brand's vision and message of luxury.
  3. Match the icon with different fonts to see which combinations work best.
  4. Select your favorite logo.
  5. Show it to friends and family to get feedback regarding the icon, color scheme, and font.

What are some candle logo examples?

What should be on my scented candle logo?

  • Your business name in an original yet bold typeface, such as Biryani, Candal, or Denk One.
  • An icon that matches your brand identity, like a candle or flame.
  • A monochromatic color scheme, or a range of colors that will pop.
