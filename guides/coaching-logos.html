How do I make a coaching logo?

  1. Decide on a color palette that will convey the emotions you want to be associated with.
  2. Find a typeface that's easy to read and captures a key element of your brand.
  3. For icons, pick a single idea that will resonate with your company's messaging.
  4. Use a logo maker and experiment with your ideas.
  5. Pick your favorite design and download it.

What makes a coaching logo good?

A good coaching logo should be eye-catching while conveying your brand identity. For a memorable design, opt for vibrant colors like green or orange, along with a bold typeface like Nunito Sans or Heebo to help your business name stand out. Round off the logo with an inspiring icon, such as a person lifting weights.
