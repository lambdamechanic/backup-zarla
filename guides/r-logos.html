FAQs :

How do I design an "R" logo?

  1. Research "R" letterform logos to see what has been done and where you can create a fresh look.
  2. Consider the typeface and what it says about your brand.
  3. Choose a color palette that will enhance the typeface and support your brand message.
  4. Use a logo maker to create some great options.
  5. Show your options to friends and family for feedback.
  6. Download your favorite logo.

What are some great "R" logo ideas?

Great ideas for your "R" logo depend on the brand message you want to convey. Trendy typefaces like Monsier La Doulaise or Redressed pair well with purple, gold, and black to convey luxury. On the other hand, fun typefaces like Slackey or Freckle Face look great in yellow, orange, pink, and light blue to convey youthful energy and joy.
