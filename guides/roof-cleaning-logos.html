What makes a great roof cleaning logo?

A great roof cleaning logo should clearly convey your brand by using an image of a roof or someone on a roof with cleaning supplies. Blue and green are most commonly used in cleaning logos, but don't be afraid to switch up your color scheme to stand out from the crowd. Finally, round out your design with a simple, clean typeface such as Poppins or Sen.
