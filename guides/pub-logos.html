FAQs:

How to design a pub logo?

  1. Identify your target audience and the theme you will use for your logo.
  2. Look at competitors' logos for inspiration.
  3. Use a logo maker to generate unique ideas.
  4. Choose icons, colors, and typefaces that reflect the character of your business.
  5. Show your favorite designs to your inner circle and get feedback.
  6. Choose the best logo and download it.

Can I use funny pub logos for my business?

Yes, a funny pub logo suits a brand with a more relaxed, casual aesthetic. A funny pub logo can be inviting, but you need to be sure the logo doesn't go over into being vulgar or so obscure that people don't get the joke.

What should an English pub logo look like?

When designing an English pub logo, try a traditional animal-themed business name that can be used as the icon of your logo. Combine this with warm colors, such as reds, or more traditional blues and white, as well as a strong, Gothic typeface like Sancreek for a good, traditional English pub look.

Is there a pub logo font?

Pub logo fonts come in a range of different styles, from handwritten to old woodblock styles. The one you choose should complement the theme of your pub, so try fonts like Moister, Tavern, Sancreek, Playball, or Connemara Old Style.
