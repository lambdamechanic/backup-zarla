What are some good sleek logo fonts to use?

Your sleek logo's font will depend on your business's aesthetic. For a modern look, use a sans serif font like Oswald Medium or Antonio Regular. If your brand is all about style and sophistication, opt for an elegant font, such as Pacifico Regular. For a professional and structured appearance, a font like Judson Bold will work well. Have a look at our examples for inspiration.

How do I create a modern sleek logo?

  1. Think about what makes your business modern and sleek. Is it your brand, products, or target market?
  2. Get inspired by existing sleek logos and identify common colors, fonts, and icons used.
