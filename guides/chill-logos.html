What's a chill icon I can use for my logo?

An icon that's considered to be "chill" typically conveys a sense of joy, relaxation, or hipness. This could include natural imagery, such as sunshine, animals, and plants, or it could be more trendy, like headphones, gaming consoles, or sunglasses. Whichever icon you prefer to use, make sure it speaks to the nature of your business.
