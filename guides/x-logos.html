  3. Consider the typeface your "X" will appear in and how it will enhance the design.

What are some good "X" logo ideas?

Using a slightly off-balance typeface, such as Gochi Hand, creates intrigue and keeps the simple lettermark from being static. You can also try typefaces like Sacramento and Bungee that use letters to reflect an idea or feeling. A bright background with a white letter will stand out, while monochromatic palettes create a sense of understated elegance.
