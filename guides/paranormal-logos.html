What makes a good paranormal logo design?

Good paranormal logos include gothic elements, such as edgy imagery, dark color palettes, and haunting fonts to create an alluring design. Atma and Nosifer are great examples of typefaces that leave a lasting impression, thanks to their spooky charm. Colors like black and gray are accentuated by pops of red, which conjure images of blood and gore. Have a look at our examples for inspiration.

How do I come up with a paranormal logo?

  1. Consider your target market, branding plans, and the type of items or services you'll offer.
  2. Research the paranormal and gothic styles, and see what elements your competitors have used.
  3. Use a logo maker to spark your imagination.
  4. Ask trusted peers for advice and host online polls with potential customers.
  5. Choose the logo design that received the best feedback.
