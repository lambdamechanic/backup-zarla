What elements work well in a logging logo?

Wood textures, trees, chainsaws, and axes are all great images to use in a logging logo. Use natural colors, such as greens and browns to convey a connection to nature or vibrant colors for a bolder effect. Opt for solid typefaces like Saira Condensed or Josefin Sans to give your business name a strong and powerful presence.
