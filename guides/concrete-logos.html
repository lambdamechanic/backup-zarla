What is a concrete logo?

A concrete logo is used by a business that manufactures and/or sells concrete for use in industrial buildings and construction businesses.

What are some great concrete logo ideas?

Great concrete logos should be solid, grounded, and strong to show customers that your concrete is high-quality and trustworthy. Try dark blue and gray tones combined with strong building elements, such as concrete blocks, bridges, and pylons. Round off your design with a geometric typeface, such as Blinker, or add some contrast with a smooth, flowing typeface, such as Rosarivo.
