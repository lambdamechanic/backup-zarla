FAQs:

How can I make a music entertainment logo?

  1. Define the type of entertainment that your business offers.
  2. Draw inspiration from the tools, instruments, or culture of your trade.
  3. Research to see how similar businesses have designed their logos.
  4. Use Zarla's logo maker to refine your entertainment logo ideas.

What imagery should I use in my entertainment logo?

Musical iconography can include records, music symbols, audio bars, and instruments while a film or television company will draw in the imagery of cameras, televisions, and film reels. More abstract imagery, such as birds or natural elements, make for a sophisticated and more subtle design and convey notions of freedom and imagination. Take a look at our logo collection for inspiration.

What are some ideas for an entertainment TV logo?

  • Televisions combine red, green, and blue to create all the colors that we see on our screens.
  • Old-school 3D glasses used red and blue filters to achieve their effect.
  • Look to literal iconographies such as televisions, film stock, and cameras.
  • Abstract symbols such as mountains, animals, or geometric forms are versatile and intriguing.

What are the best colors for an entertainment logo design?

Bright primary colors work well to grab attention, while black and white or gray scale designs are more understated and sophisticated. A dash of color will bring your design to life and add a subtle flair to your monochromatic logo. Be bold with your color choice, but try and stick to one to three colors to keep your design clean and elegant.
