FAQs:

What is the meaning of a circle logo?

Circle logos project strength, inclusivity, and commitment in a softer, more friendly, and more lighthearted way than logos using angular shapes. If you're designing a circle logo for your brand, take a look at our examples for inspiration.

Why do companies use circular logos?

Companies use circular logos because circles represent a range of positive things, such as strength, unity, commitment, and life.
