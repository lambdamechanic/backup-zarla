How can I come up with an edgy logo design?

  1. Write down some key aspects of your business that make it edgy.
  2. Do some research and make a note of the colors, imagery, and typefaces used by other edgy brands.
  3. Use a logo maker to produce a range of edgy logo templates.
  4. Share your favorite template with trusted peers and request their honest feedback.
  5. Refine your design based on the feedback and download it.
