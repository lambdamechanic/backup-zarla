What makes a great internet logo?

A great internet logo will communicate your business identity in simple visual ways. An online shopping website might benefit from icons of the products you sell, while an internet security company might want to use color and typefaces that project strength and trust.

Why should my internet logo be in PNG or vector format?

If you save a logo in a PNG format, it can be used on a variety of digital platforms. Unlike JPEG images, PNG images can be saved with a transparent background, making them ideal for online use.
