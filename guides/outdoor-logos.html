How can I make an outdoor brand logo?

Consider the type of outdoor activity or service that your business will offer. For example, a mountain bike trail business will lend itself to a more edgy design, bolder colors, and energetic imagery, while softer more understated imagery and elegant fonts might work better for an outdoor camping business.

What are some ideas for an outdoor company logo?

Classic outdoor logos work well with organic colors like greens, browns, and blues that capture the elements of nature. For a more out-of-the-box design, try juxtaposing an elegant white design with a bright and bold background color like orange, yellow, or purple. Be inspired by natural imagery like plants, leaves, trees, and landscapes.

What makes a good outdoor adventure logo?

Your outdoor adventure logo should be exciting and bold, calling your customers to action. Bright colors can give your design vibrance and energy. Italicized fonts can create a sense of movement and action. Silhouettes of rock climbers, mountain bikers, or trail runners are simple and elegant but create a sense of excitement and fun.
