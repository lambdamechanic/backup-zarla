Why should I use a black and white logo brand?

A black and white logo is clean and minimalistic. It works very well for use in printing and for advertising partnerships and collaborations. A black and white color palette also forces designers to focus on simplifying the elements of the logo, which makes for easier resizing.

What is the meaning of a black and white logo?

Black and white logos are minimalistic, practical, and timeless. They can generally be used to portray elements of sophistication, luxury, strength, and authority.
