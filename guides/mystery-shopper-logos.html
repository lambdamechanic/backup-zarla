What makes a good mystery shopper logo design?

A good mystery shopper logo should successfully capture the essence of a mystery shopper's role through familiar imagery of shopping items. The strategic use of assertive and formal typefaces, such as Cinzel or Saira Condensed, and sophisticated colors will help elevate the shopping imagery.
