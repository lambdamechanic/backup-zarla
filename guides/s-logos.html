FAQs:

How do I create an "S" logo?

  1. Write down some key points about your business and its values.
  2. Think of symbols that represent your company's unique offerings and try to fashion them into the shape of the letter "S."
  3. Use our free logo maker to come up with a few ideas for your "S" logo.
  4. Pick your favorite ideas and tweak them until you're happy.
  5. Show your family and friends and ask them what they think.
  6. Use their feedback to narrow your final selection.
  7. Pick your favorite logo and download it.

What are some ideas for cool "S" logos?

A cool "S" logo will communicate your unique brand identity in the shape of the letter "S." Think of the products you sell or the people you sell to, and try and incorporate the images and colors that come to mind into an "S." Using typography that will give your "S" logo a 3D effect will make it even cooler.

If you really want to be creative, try placing your "S" in different positions and orientations. What effect does tilting it forward a bit create? What happens if you flip it on its side and have it pass through another "S," making a cross? The more creative you get, the more memorable and personal your "S" logo will be.

How do I make an "S" logo for gaming?

  1. List the images and colors that come to mind when thinking about your character or avatar.
  2. Think about how you can use those images to fashion the letter "S." For example, if your game features ropes, use those to make an "S" shape.
  3. Try to make it as unique to your brand as possible. The "S" can act as an identifier and can be stylized and used in various typefaces and sizes to differentiate it from competing "S" logos.
