FAQs:

How can I design an obscure logo?

  1. Research some famous obscure logos to get an idea of what works.
  2. Review your business plan and what your brand means to you.
  3. Think about your inspiration for the business and how you want people to perceive your brand.
  4. Think of icons that hold hidden meanings or symbols.
  5. Consider colors that represent your brand.
  6. Find fonts that complement your style.
  7. Use a logo maker to combine your elements and come up with the perfect design.

Where can I download obscure logos for free?

What is an obscure logo?

An obscure logo has a hidden meaning or it may have a hidden element that holds significance for the company in question. Typically, you would only understand the hidden meaning if you understand the key or spot the hidden element.
