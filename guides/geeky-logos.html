What makes a logo geeky?

Geeky logos use imagery and graphical elements that are associated with eccentric and non-mainstream hobbies, passions, and interests. Common geeky elements include oversized glasses, video game controllers, books and comic strips, and technology-related imagery such as computers and circuitry.

How can I come up with a geeky logo design?

  1. Consider the nature of your business and who your target market will be.
  2. Be inspired by the logos of existing companies and competing brands.
  3. Do some research to find icons and imagery that will match your brand identity.
  4. Use a logo maker to generate unique design options.
  5. Refine your logo until you've crafted the perfect image for your business.
