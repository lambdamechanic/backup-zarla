What does an umbrella logo mean?

An umbrella logo uses a real-life or abstract umbrella icon to portray a business that manufactures or sells umbrellas. However, an umbrella is also a symbol of safety and protection, so can be used by security or insurance companies. It could also be used by a large corporation that has many different branches.

What should I remember when printing my umbrella logo?

When designing your umbrella logo for printing, you should ensure that your logo text is clear and legible and that your design does not have more than three different colors. A good, simple icon will also be beneficial. Consider downloading your logo in PNG format, as it will then have a transparent background which is best for printing and will look good on a variety of marketing materials, such as shirts.
