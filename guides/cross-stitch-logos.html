How do I design a cross-stitch logo?

  1. Review your business plan for design ideas.
  2. Research your top competitors' logos to see what works.
  3. Think about your brand specialty and how to convey that.
  4. Use a logo maker to experiment with colors, icons, and typefaces.
  5. Design a few logo options and ask friends and family for feedback.
  6. Tweak the best option for your brand and download it.

What icon should I use in my cross-stitch logo design?

Sewing icons, such as a needle and thread, scissors, or spools of thread, work well for cross-stitch logos, as do images of the completed products you make and sell. However, possibly the best option for a cross-stitch icon is one that uses a cross-stitch pattern to form the image.
