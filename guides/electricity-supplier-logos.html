What are some good electrical logo symbols?

When designing an electrical logo, you can use classic symbols such as lightning bolts, lightbulbs, lamps, and electrical wiring, which will all highlight your niche and make it clear to customers what it is you do.

Can I use a utility logo for my electricity supply business?

Yes, electricity is considered a utility, so you can use a utility logo for your business. Instead of focusing on just an electrical-based icon when designing your utility logo, consider adding icons that reference electricity, heat, heating, and water.
