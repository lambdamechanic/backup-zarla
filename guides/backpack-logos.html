How do I create a backpack logo?

What are great backpack logo images?

The imagery you use in your backpack logo should reflect the quality of your products and showcase its best features. For example, if you're creating trustworthy and durable backpacks for hikers, include an icon with a hiker and their backpack to highlight these themes. Alternatively, you could include a comical or trendy mascot to show creativity.
