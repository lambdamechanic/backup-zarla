What are some good icons for a Spanish restaurant logo?

Restaurants that pride themselves on spicier meals may want to include icons such as chilis and/or flames. Those that specialize in great drinks should consider icons that include beverages like sangria. If you're aiming to create a happy ambiance by encouraging dancing, consider using vibrantly colored icons that offer a sense of festivity.
