FAQs:

Where can I find some creative handmade logo ideas?

Take a look at our collection of handmade logos for inspiration. If you see a design that you like, simply click on the image to tweak the colors, icons, and fonts. Alternatively, you can use our logo maker to create a logo from scratch.

How can I come up with a handmade shop logo?

  1. Do some research to find some handmade design elements that you like.
  2. Draw inspiration from the types of products that you sell.
  3. Use a free logo maker to create a range of design options.
  4. Pick your favorite design and request some feedback from family and friends.
  5. Refine the design based on the feedback and download it.

Where can I download a handmade products logo for free?

You can create, edit, and download free handmade product logos by clicking on "Edit" beneath one of our examples, or by using our logo maker.

What elements work well in a handmade logo?

Typefaces such as Gochi Hand, Amatica SC, and Sacramento have a handwritten aesthetic and work well to add a personable appeal to logos. Natural colors, such as browns, greens, and blues will also imbue your design with a down-to-earth quality. Simple icons constructed with hand-drawn linework work well to give logos a personalized touch.
