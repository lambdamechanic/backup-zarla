What makes a good childcare logo?

A great childcare logo will communicate a brand niche in simple visual ways. A toddler childcare company might feature dancing children, while a warm and comfortable baby makes an ideal emblem for a childcare center that specializes in the care of babies, for example. Consider your typeface and whether you want a fun and playful brand that kids love, or a professional and reliable brand that parents trust.
