FAQs:

What is a geometric logo?

Geometric logos feature circles, squares, rectangles, and triangles. They can either be used in their basic, immediately recognizable forms, in organic ways that draw inspiration from nature and the world around us, or as abstract representations of their respective characteristics. Take a look at our examples for inspiration.
