FAQs:

What is a whimsical logo?

Whimsical logos use bright colors, playful icons, and bold fonts to create a spirit of childlike freedom and spontaneity. These types of logos work well for companies that have younger target markets. Check out our examples for inspiration.

How do I create a whimsical logo?

  1. Look at the logos of brands that inspire creativity, adventure, and spontaneity, and make a note of the icons, colors, and fonts they've used.
  2. Think about the ideas and feelings you'd like to communicate with your logo.
  3. Start playing around with logo designs and colors.
  4. Use a logo maker to make it easier.
  5. Choose your best logo and download it.
