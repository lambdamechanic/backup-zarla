What makes a good cup business logo design?

Your cup business logo should be clear, fun, and distinctly capture the essence of your product. Draw inspiration from the usage of your cups. Opt for colors that are visually pleasing and that will fit with your brand's message. Bright colors are associated with a fun product, while dark colors speak of a more serious product.
