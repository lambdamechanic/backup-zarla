What should a dark logo like?

Typically, a dark logo should consist of at least one dark color, such as navy blue or black. It should be used as the backdrop, or contrast a light icon and text color, such as white, red, or yellow. Icons can range from organic to geometric, depending on your brand. The logo's typeface should be legible and versatile, such as Sen or Cinzel.
