How do I create a magazine logo?

  1. Think about the kind of content you intend to publish and look for inspiration from competing magazines.
  2. Consider your target audience and how you will use your logo.
  3. Use a logo maker to experiment with different combinations of graphic elements.
  4. Test your favorite designs on the magazine cover and on social media to make an informed decision.
  5. Ask for feedback from potential subscribers.
  6. Use the feedback to tweak and download the best design.

How do I choose a magazine logo font?

Depending on your target audience and the type of content you'll be publishing, you can choose from the most diverse original fonts. The calligraphic font Parisienne and the graceful typeface Crushed will be ideal for women's magazines, while the quirky fonts Marko One and Fredoka One would work well for children's magazines. Serious trade publications would benefit from a professional font, such as Bebas Neue or Oswald.
