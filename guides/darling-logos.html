What makes a logo darling?

A logo could be considered "darling" if it conveys feelings of charm and sweetness. Its icon could resemble things that are seen as "cute," such as kittens, cupcakes, and butterflies. Colors like pink, purple, blue, or green could add a hint of fun, depending on its shade. A lovely typeface, such as Ranchers or Pacifico, would round off the logo.
