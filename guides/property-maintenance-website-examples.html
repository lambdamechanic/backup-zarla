Property Maintenance Website Examples - 5 In-Depth Reviews of High-Quality Sites

These 5 property maintenance website examples effectively showcase the value of property care and restoration services with impactful images and content.
By Carla Lottering, Jul 25, 2024
Carla is a Copywriter for Zarla based in Cape Town, South Africa. She has over 5 years' experience in online content creation and writes extensively on logos for small businesses.

Property maintenance websites should depict the company's specialist services in an engaging manner with vibrant images of real projects, descriptive text, and client reviews to convey reliability and professionalism. Information about awards and other recognitions, and online chat features, are great additions.

How We Evaluate Websites:

Zarla checks that sites meet basic web standards for mobile and desktop. We also use Google's PageSpeed Insights, and WAVE, a service that evaluates accessibility for individuals with disabilities. Aspect Maintenance Services

https://www.aspect.co.uk/

London, England, U.K.

Aspect Maintenance Services provides 24/7 property maintenance solutions to the local community, including plumbing, electric, and roofing. The website's hero image of a speedy company truck denotes ideas of professionalism and energy, effectively conveying the company's commitment to fast and reliable services.

The home page features an availability search bar for visitors to easily find specific information, such as the company's account services, industry accreditations, and various contact options. The large selection of prominent CTA buttons encourages optimal user engagement.

Pros and Cons

The inclusion of a comprehensive pricing page promotes transparency and trustworthiness. The website also includes a web accessibility button. WAVE picked up no contrast errors, enhancing the overall user experience.

The website could benefit from more personable imagery to complement its personalized services. There could also be an interactive map to help users more easily find the company's location coverage.

Desktop Review

The desktop website has a minimalistic appeal that feels both professional and engaging. While most pages are text-heavy, there is a robust selection of information available, covering both important and interesting topics. According to Google's PageSpeed Insights test, the desktop site has fast loading times and a high performance score of 95/100.

Mobile Review

The mobile website features a responsive design that slims the images and content down to fit most screens for a user-friendly experience. The sticky top menu bar prominently displays the company logo and booking CTA button, promoting a cohesive and interactive user experience. However, the site's PageSpeed performance score is lower than its desktop counterpart at 47/100.

HOA Services

https://hoafixit.com/

Tigard, Oregon, U.S.

HOA Services is a U.S.-based company that provides residential and commercial maintenance, repairs, and janitorial services. The website greets visitors with a clean and professional aesthetic, while easy site navigation is ensured with a search icon to help users find specific information.

The "Process" page features informative content on the company's working process and introduces prospective clients to their ethos of high-quality services and craftsmanship.

Pros and Cons

The content-rich home page effectively advertises the company's services, while the customer reviews section lends credibility. According to WAVE, the site does not have many contrast errors.

While the top menu bar is sticky, the top-right CTA button's text could be displayed in a more vibrant color for additional prominence. The repetition of images across the website is noticeable. However, the company name and the website URL are slightly different, which may indicate a lack of coherency.

Desktop Review

The desktop site is well-spaced with ample breathing room for the images and text to pop. The addition of high-quality images to the various page links in the top menu bar adds a personable touch. While the site does not have a traditional online enquiry form, its "Maintenance Shield" feature provides users with a comprehensive overview of the company's proposal process.

Mobile Review

The mobile site does not have the same impact as the desktop version, with the hero image getting cut off and the top menu bar not remaining sticky as you scroll down. However, users can easily access the company's contact information and use the search icon to find pertinent information. The mobile site's performance scored 61/100 on Google's PageSpeed Insights test.

At Your Service Property Maintenance

https://at-your-service.com.au/

Sutherland, New South Wales, Australia

At Your Service Property Maintenance specializes in both commercial and residential home and garden maintenance solutions. Unlike some of its competitors, the website takes a more elegant approach to advertising the company's services with a refined color scheme, along with a sophisticated heading typeface and a good selection of modern, high-quality images.

While the site does not have a client reviews section, which is typically seen on competitor websites, it has a content-rich "Client Stories" page with before and after images that give prospective customers a good overview of the company's services.

Pros and Cons

The website's hero image depicts a stylish and neat property that may be indicative of the effectiveness of the company's services. This idea is also reflected in the tagline "Making Life Easier." The online enquiry process is seamless and the FAQs section on the "Services" pages is a helpful addition for potential clients interested in the company's pricing structure.

According to WAVE, there are several contrast errors between the top menu bar's text and the hero image, albeit there is an accessibility adjustments tool. The site does not feature any of the company's health and safety certifications, which may impart a sense of trustworthiness.

Desktop Review

The desktop website's performance score of 91/100 on Google's PageSpeed Insights test is well-deserved, reflecting a pleasant user experience with well-spaced page layouts, prominent CTA buttons, and an attractive interface. Site pages load quite quickly and visitors benefit from an accessibility tools menu, which includes options to increase the text size or change the font.

Mobile Review

The mobile site slims down the images and text without losing resolution or readability, albeit the hero image gets cut off which seems like an oversite for a mobile design. Like its counterpart, the mobile website does not have a sticky top menu bar, but the helpful accessibility adjustments tool is always visible on the page. It scored 64/100 in the performance category on PageSpeed.

The Trusted Toolbox

https://thetrustedtoolbox.com/

Norcross, Georgia, U.S.

The Trusted Toolbox offers home repair, remodeling, and maintenance services to Atlanta-based clients. The website effectively balances elements of sophistication and power with a high-quality hero image of a remodeled kitchen and a bright color scheme that brings a sense of energy to the site.

The home page is feature-rich with various trust badges that denote credibility, while the location map is a helpful addition for users wanting check if they fall under the company's coverage. Financing services for projects are also available, which is a standout feature.

Pros and Cons

The handyman services gallery is visually appealing with before and after images. Users can also schedule an estimate or chat with an expert via the messaging tool in the site's bottom-right corner.

WAVE picked up a large number of contrast errors on the website. The "About Us" page's images are small and pixelated, distracting from the impactful text. The addition of engaging walkthrough videos or other visual elements could enhance the overall user experience.

Desktop Review

On Google's PageSpeed Insights test, the desktop site's performance is well-scored at 96/100 as seen on real-world tests. This is thanks to its fast page loading times, accessibility menu, and comprehensive page links. The site's minimalistic page layout provides a balanced aesthetic and contributes to a pleasant user experience, as does its intuitive navigation.

Mobile Review

While the mobile site keeps images and text well-spaced and crisp, the hero image gets cut off and distracts from the otherwise attractive interface. The site's performance is still well-scored, albeit lower than its counterpart at 75/100. In real-world tests, we found that the mobile website performed well on most devices and the speed was within acceptable limits.

Blue Sky Property Services

https://blueskypropertyservices.ca/

Millgrove, Ontario, Canada

Blue Sky Property Services provides end-to-end property maintenance services, including snow removal, landscaping, and gardening. The website does not compromise functionality for aesthetics, ensuring visitors can easily navigate its pages and access important information regarding the company's services, ethos, and contact options.

However, the site could move some of its more engaging content to the home page to give users a better overview of the company at-a-glance. It could also benefit from a photo gallery with before and after images.

Pros and Cons

The site combines minimal content with crisp images to represent the business' ethos of precision and refinement. The client testimonials section and company statistics on the "About" page instill confidence.

The website performs quite poorly on WAVE's accessibility test, which indicates various contrast errors on the home page. The CTA buttons could also be displayed more prominently for better user engagement.

Desktop Review

The desktop site uses pops of bright colors and a unique hero video to attract visitors in search of specific maintenance services. Placing an emphasis on simplicity and modern visual elements, the site does a good job of conveying the company's commitment to no-fuss, high-quality workmanship.

The website has a high performance score of 95/100 on Google's PageSpeed Insights test.

Mobile Review

The home page's hero video plays without fault on the mobile site, but the company logo is quite small and less impactful than on the desktop site. While it doesn't distract from the user experience, it's easily fixed and would provide a quick visual navigation tool. The site's PageSpeed performance score is much lower than the desktop version at 54/100.
