FAQs:

What is a playful logo?

A playful logo is a design that usually consists of a fun color, a cute icon, and a charming font that ties the design together. These logos typically use blue, pink, or green to convey a sense of playfulness, coupled with a sweet icon, like baby animals or stars. Cartoonish typefaces, such as Ribeye or Gochi Hand, will also highlight the logo's whimsy.

Where can I find playful designs?

You can use Zarla's logo maker to create and download playful logo designs for free. Alternatively, see our collection of playful logos for inspiration.

How do I make a playful logo?

  1. Think about what makes your business playful. Is it your products, services, or aesthetics?
  2. Get inspired by existing playful logos and identify common colors, icons, and fonts used.
  3. Use a logo maker to produce a variety of logo ideas.
  4. Play around with different design elements until you have the perfect logo.

Which companies have playful logos?

  • Pringles.
  • Nickelodeon.
  • Toys R Us.
  • Barbie.
  • Snapchat.

How do I design a playful modern logo?

To create a playful logo that's modern, make either the color scheme, icon, or font the focal point of the design. If your color scheme is bright, opt for a neat font, such as Lusitana Bold. If your icon is loud, a monochromatic color like blue or black will work well. If your typeface choice is cartoonish, go for an icon with clean linework.
