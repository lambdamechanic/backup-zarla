Is brown a good color for logos?

While brown may be considered to be dull or boring by some, it is actually a strong color choice that can convey strength, durability, quality, and honesty, and is thus a good color choice for logos.

When used in logos, brown can represent nature, comfort, friendship, and nurturing, as well as strength, confidence, security, and reliability.
