FAQs:

What are some tips for creating a charity logo?

  1. Write down keywords and concepts that relate to your charity and your goals as an NPO.
  2. Try out different combinations of colors, choose symbols that are descriptive and memorable, and compare different typefaces and fonts.
  3. Get a few options together and show them to your friends and family.
  4. You can also view our charity logo ideas for inspiration or use our logo maker to create your own design.

What is the best color for charity logos?

The best color for your charity logo will depend on a lot of different things. In general, you'll want to go for softer, pastel colors and use shades that are associated with peace, kindness, energy, and action. Some good choices for a charity logo's color include blue, green, orange, yellow, and purple.
